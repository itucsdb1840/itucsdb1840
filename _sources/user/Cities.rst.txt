Cities
==========

**Any visitor, without logging in, has the ability to:**

Examine the city data where each city is displayed with it's respective:

* Name
* Number of total attacks targeting it
* Number of total fatalities sustained
* Number of total injuries sustained
* Most Common Attack Target
* Most Common Attack Type
