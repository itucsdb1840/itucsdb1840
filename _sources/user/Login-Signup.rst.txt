Login-Signup
============
**Users are able to login or sign up using the form in the home page**
      .. figure:: /figures/login.png
**Rules for signing up are:**

* You must have a unique username
* Your username can't contain the following characters: '(' ')' ';'
* Your username or password can't be blank

**After the user logs in the form will hide itself**

.. figure:: /figures/loginHide.png
